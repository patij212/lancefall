## Best-in-class push (v2.1) — depth, variety, feel
Goal: make it the best in its category. Driven by build-depth, in-run variety, and game-feel — the three things the genre's best win on. Each feature implemented → verified (tests + tsc + build + in-browser) → committed; capped by a 2-agent adversarial review (correctness + balance) whose findings were all fixed.

- **Perk evolutions — build-defining fusion capstones.** Stack the right perks high enough and a fusion EVOLUTION unlocks, offered as a guaranteed glowing slot in the next draft (Hades duo-boon style). 6: IMPALER (longreach3+pierce2), SUPERNOVA (chain3+nova2), PERPETUAL (siphon2+timethief1), WRAITH (afterimage2+longreach2), INFERNO (grazeburn2+slipstream1), JUGGERNAUT (secondwind2+chain1). Additive — keep the source perks, stack a dramatic bonus applied LAST in deriveStats (new 4th `evoApply` param). An "EVOLUTION READY" announce fires when a recipe is first met; evolutions thread into the run debrief + share string + a HOW TO PLAY codex. `evolutions.ts` (new, +tests), `perks.ts`, `world.ts`, `game.ts`, `ui.ts`, `style.css`.
- **Elite "Champion" enemies.** A small intensity-scaled chance (capped 2 on screen, none in the first ~minute) promotes an eligible enemy to a Champion: 3× HP, 1.45× size, a pulsing gold crown aura, a "CHAMPION inbound" moment. Killing one rains 6 bonus shard gems + 800 score but detonates a 12-bullet volatile ring — risk on the reward. Mobile/fair singles only (darter/orbiter/lancer/bomber). `tune.ts`, `types.ts`, `world.ts`, `waves.ts` (+tests), `game.ts`, `render.ts`.
- **Game-feel pass.** Dash afterimage trail — crisp ship-silhouette echoes streaking along the dash, growing/brightening toward the tip (the genre's signature "snap"). Dash hits that don't kill now emit an impact spark (gold for champions) so the spear visibly bites tanky enemies. `render.ts`, `game.ts`.
- **Discoverability.** HOW TO PLAY upgraded from a toast to a real modal: 7 mechanics rules + all 6 evolution recipes so players know the build goals to chase. `ui.ts`, `style.css`.
- **Adversarial review + fixes.** 2-agent review (correctness + balance). Fixed: WRAITH afterimage faded wrong (alpha hardcoded /0.5 clamped for >half its life); rollDraftCards off-by-one; IMPALER over-tuned (+3→+2 dmg); PERPETUAL degenerate infinite-dash/permanent-slowmo (refund/regen/slowmo all curbed); WRAITH underwhelming (buffed to its 4-pick cost); elite HP 4×→3×; elite bloomer removed (a tanky turret = un-fun sponge). 115 tests, clean build, zero console errors. `evolutions.ts`, `render.ts`, `tune.ts`, `waves.ts`.
- **Combo heat vignette.** A combo-tier-coloured glow swells at the screen edges from ×10 up (full by ~×70) — euphoric escalation for the core chaining loop. Edges only (inner half-radius clear) so bullet readability is untouched; gated by reduce-flashing. `render.ts`.
- **Riposte — the roster's first defensive perk.** Your dash shatters enemy bullets in a band around the spear (boss shots excepted, so boss fights stay honest); widens per stack; no stamina refund (utility, not an infinite-dash engine). 10 → 11 perks. `perks.ts` (+test), `game.ts` (resolveDashHits), `ui.ts`.

## v3 — "best in its category" expansion (multi-agent workflow driven)
A 5-architect design workflow + tech-lead synthesis produced a sequenced, conflict-aware plan (`V3_PLAN.md`); built sequentially, each step verified + committed; design and review done with dynamic workflows.

- **Phase 0 — shared groundwork.** Save schema versioning (`migrate.ts`, one-time legacy-key migration, zero loss) + a `postApply` stat-pipeline capstone (so heat/relics/mutators compose without fighting over `deriveStats`). Canonical order: base→meta→mutator→ship→perks→evo→postApply→boons.
- **Phase 1 — content.** New enemies **Drifter** (arc-fan zoner) + **Shade** (teleporting ambusher) → 9 archetypes. 5th boss **THE HOLLOW** — an intangible phantom you can only damage by dashing through it during telegraphed Clone-Sync windows, with killable echo clones. Arena → 13-wave/5-boss gauntlet; Boss Rush → 5 bosses; both climax on the Hollow.
- **Phase 2 — variety.** 6 **run mutators** (the Daily picks 1-2 deterministically — Champion Tide / Glass Cannon / Bullet Storm / Fog of War / Berserk / Windfall) + 5 **mid-run events** (Shrine / Gambler / Treasure / Champion Hunt / Cursed Pact, pause-and-choose on a ~75s cadence).
- **Phase 3 — endgame depth.** 🔥 **Heat ascension** (8 levels, up to ×3.2 score), ◈ **build archetypes** (draft-biasing build paths, zero-extra-rng so Daily stays deterministic), ☠ **cursed relics** (6 double-edged draft items on the same chassis), ⧬ **build DNA** (shareable build codec). Ghost-replay cut by the synthesis. Capped by a 2-agent review that caught a **critical dash-lock** (relic+mutator could make dashing unaffordable → now clamped) + balance fixes.
- **Phase 4 — Steam polish.** Adaptive perf (frame-time particle auto-scale), combo-blazing dash trail (4→6 ghosts, whitening at high combo), and a victory-cinematic money moment (concentric shockwaves + fountain + punch-zoom).

- **THE SOVEREIGN — the 6th & final boss.** A crowned monarch that warps space. Its body is ARMORED (dash deals nothing) while its three orbiting Cores live; dash a Core to shatter it (combo + score + a weak-point chunk to the crown). Break all three → the body cracks EXPOSED for a punish window, then the Cores reform. Every Sovereign bullet curves under a gravity well, bending volleys into sweeping galaxy arms. - **CLUTCH moments.** **LAST BREATH** — an automatic bullet-time second wind: when a lethal hit lands and it's off cooldown, the game drops into a deep 0.18× slow-mo for 1.7s, grants 0.55s grace, and shoves nearby bullets outward to open an escape lane — it doesn't save you, it gives you the time to save yourself (38s cooldown; sits ahead of the Second Chance revive). **COMBO ERUPTION** — at ×50 / ×100 / … a player-centred nova shatters every non-boss bullet in 360px (breathing room), scorches nearby chaff, and drops a big score bonus; re-arms on a combo break. Also fixed a real hot-path allocation (`gravityPull` built an object per boss-bullet per frame → inlined at the call site, pure fn kept for tests) and the nova ring drifting off-centre under the burst's punch-zoom (now camera-projected).
